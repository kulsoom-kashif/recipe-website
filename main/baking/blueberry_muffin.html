Blueberry Muffin

Back to Baking | Home

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 2 tsp baking powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix all ingredients except blueberries.
  3. Fold in blueberries and spoon into muffin tin.
  4. Bake for 20-25 minutes.
