Banana Bread

Back to Baking | Home

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking soda

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mash bananas and mix with other ingredients.
  3. Pour into a loaf pan and bake for 50-60 minutes.
