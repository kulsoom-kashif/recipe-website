Chocolate Cake

Back to Baking | Home

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix dry ingredients together.
  3. Add wet ingredients and mix until smooth.
  4. Pour into a greased pan and bake for 30-35 minutes.
