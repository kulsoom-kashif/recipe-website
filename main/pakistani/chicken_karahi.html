Chicken Karahi

Back to Pakistani | Home

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger & garlic
  • Spices
  • Green chilies

Instructions

  1. Cook chicken with ginger, garlic, and spices.
  2. Add tomatoes and cook until oil separates.
  3. Garnish with green chilies and serve.
