Sweet and Sour Chicken

Back to Chinese | Home

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Pineapple chunks
  • Bell peppers
  • Vinegar
  • Soy sauce
  • Sugar
  • Cornstarch

Instructions

  1. Cut chicken into pieces and coat with cornstarch.
  2. Fry chicken until golden.
  3. Stir-fry vegetables, add pineapple and sauce ingredients.
  4. Combine chicken and sauce, cook until thickened.
