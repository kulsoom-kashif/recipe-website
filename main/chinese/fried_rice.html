Fried Rice

Back to Chinese | Home

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Eggs
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Soy sauce
  • Green onions

Instructions

  1. Scramble eggs and set aside.
  2. Stir-fry vegetables, add rice and soy sauce.
  3. Mix in eggs and green onions, cook until heated through.
